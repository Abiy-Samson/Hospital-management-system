Functionalities
===============
- Laboratory Management
- Patient Registration
- Keep Track of Patient Electronic Health Record History
- Pharmacy Management
- X-Ray Management

Steps
=====
1. Create the database. It is in the 'sql' folder.
2. Check your database configuration in 'application/configs/database.php'.
3. Now you can browse the web application.
4. To sign up for a user account, change your browser location to 'http://localhost/labmgmt/index.php/account/signup'. Make sure that the first user to register must be of role admin.
